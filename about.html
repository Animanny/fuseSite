About

The Issue

Learning in the traditional confines of a classroom is no longer enough to generate well-prepared graduates. We believe that an outdated system is insufficient when trying to prepare students for careers that may not even exist yet.

The Mission

Through a hands-on curriculum, we teach students how to think, not what​ to think. In doing so, FUSE hopes to bridge the growing gap between business education and the real world.

The Vision

Regardless of what careers our students choose to pursue, we believe that the FUSE lessons extend far beyond the business world. The analytical, cognitive, and presentation skills members gain are invaluable assets that are applicable to a countless range of careers. Whether they are tomorrow’s entrepreneurs, lawmakers, doctors, or engineers, our hope is that FUSE graduates will be change-makers.
